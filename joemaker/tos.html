Welcome to Joe Maker

Joe Maker is committed to providing a creative and engaging environment for all users. It's imperative that each member of our community respects these guidelines to ensure a positive and safe experience. By using Joe Maker, you agree to adhere to these terms without exception.

Acceptable Content

  • Original Creations: All content must be your own original work. This includes levels, characters, and music.
  • Appropriate Themes: Content must not contain excessive vulgarity or offensive material. We allow edgy humor, but it must not cross the line into obscenity.
  • Innovative Gameplay: We encourage creativity and challenge. Designs that are engaging and unique are highly valued.

Prohibited Content

  • Infringing Material: Do not upload content that violates intellectual property rights.
  • Offensive Content: Materials that promote hate, violence, or discrimination are strictly prohibited.
  • Illegal Activities: Content that promotes or involves illegal activities is not allowed.
  • Harassment: Do not upload content that harasses, bullies, or attacks other individuals or groups.

Enforcement and Consequences

Violations of these terms will result in immediate removal of content and may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from Joe Maker. We take these rules very seriously and expect our community to do the same.

Amendments to Terms

These terms may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our policies or in response to community feedback. It is the responsibility of each user to stay informed of any changes.

Contact and Reporting

If you have questions about these terms or wish to report a violation, please fill out our report form. We are dedicated to maintaining a respectful and lawful community.
